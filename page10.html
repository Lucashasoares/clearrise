Pressure Washing

Pressure washing is a cost-effective way to add value and extra appeal to your property. When done as part of a regularly maintenance cleaning, pressure washing prevents the accumulation of algae, mold and dirt.

Clear Rise offers professional pressure washing services for a great price to all the commercial facilities located in Calgary and neighbouring cities. Our high-quality pressure washing services will make your building’s deck, driveway, parking lot, and walls look like new again.
