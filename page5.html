Our History

Clear Rise was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to make life easier for both businesses and families by providing reliable, high-quality exterior cleaning services. For years, commercial buildings and residential communities struggled to find a company that could clean parking lots, wash windows, and unclog gutters — all in one place. That’s where we came in.

What started as a small operation quickly grew thanks to the trust and loyalty of our clients. From our very first service, Clear Rise built its reputation on professionalism, safety, and real results. Our clients didn’t just come back — they referred us to neighbors, colleagues, and property managers. Word of mouth became our engine.

Today, after more than 7 years in the industry, Clear Rise is proud to serve a growing number of homes and commercial buildings across the region. Whether it’s sparkling clean windows, pressure washing for a fresh exterior, spotless parking lots, or safe and thorough gutter cleaning — our highly trained team gets the job done.

We’re more than just a cleaning company. We’re a local team committed to excellence, safety, and helping your property look its absolute best.
