FAQ

Why should windows be cleaned at least twice a year?

Regular window cleaning prevents buildup of dirt, hard water stains, and debris. It keeps your home looking great and helps extend the life of your windows.

What is soft washing and is it different than pressure washing?

Yes, soft washing uses low pressure with cleaning solutions to gently remove dirt, mold, and mildew—ideal for delicate surfaces. Pressure washing uses high force and is better for hard surfaces like concrete.

Why should I get my house pressure washed?

Pressure washing removes built-up grime, mold, and algae that can damage your home’s exterior. It restores curb appeal and protects your property.

Why do I need to get my eavestrough cleaned?

Clogged eavestroughs can lead to water damage, foundation issues, and roof leaks. Regular cleaning ensures proper drainage and protects your home.

Why is it better to hire a local window cleaning company over a national one?

Local companies offer personalized service, faster response times, and care more about building long-term relationships in the community.

Why should gutters be cleaned by professionals?

Professional cleaners have the tools and training to remove debris safely and thoroughly, reducing the risk of damage and personal injury.

What is high rise window cleaning?

High rise window cleaning involves cleaning windows on tall buildings using ropes, lifts, or scaffolding. It requires specialized equipment and trained technicians.

Is it dangerous if I don’t clean my gutters?

Yes, blocked gutters can cause serious issues like water leaks, mold growth, and foundation damage. Regular cleaning prevents costly repairs.

Is there a difference between residential and commercial window cleaning?

Yes, commercial cleaning often involves larger buildings, specialized tools, and stricter safety standards. Residential cleaning is tailored to homes and usually done more frequently.

Do you install Christmas lights on trees?

Yes, we professionally install Christmas lights on trees and home exteriors, ensuring safe setup and stunning results.
